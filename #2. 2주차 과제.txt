1. 월별 액티브한 사용자 수 카운트 쿼리
### 결과 예시
### 2019-05 : 400
### 2019-06 : 500
### ...

### 쿼리
SELECT 
    join1.y_m,
    COUNT(*)
FROM (
    SELECT 
        DISTINCT to_char(st.ts, 'YYYY-MM') AS y_m, usc.userid
    FROM raw_data.session_timestamp AS st
    JOIN raw_data.user_session_channel AS usc
        ON st.sessionid = usc.sessionid
) AS join1
GROUP BY
    join1.y_m

### 결과
2019-05	281
2019-06	459
2019-07	623
2019-09	639
2019-10	763
2019-08	662
2019-11	721
